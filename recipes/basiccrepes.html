Basic Crepes

Description

Here is a simple but delicious crepe batter which can be made in minutes. It's made from ingredients that everyone has on hand.

Wake that someone special with a plate piled high of these deliciously simple crepes - they will ...

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ cup water
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. You can cook crepes in either a crepe pan or a 20cm (base measurement) non-stick frying pan
  2. MSift flour into a medium mixing bowl and make a well in centrePlace.
  3. Cover batter with plastic wrap and set aside at room temperature for 30 minutes
  4. TPreheat oven to 150°C. Line 2 baking trays with non-stick baking paper
  5. Use a metal spatula to gently lift the edge of crepe to ensure it is light golden and lacy underneath.
  6. To serve, fold each crepe in half and then in half again to form triangles.
